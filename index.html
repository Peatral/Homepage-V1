Just to clarify

This is just a Website to host my School-Presentations I made with reveal.js. Where does that "obsession" for reveal.js come from you ask? My computer science teacher showed us multiple times the "Death by PowerPoint" TED talk by David JP Phillips and then reveal.js to show us how good it is. Carrying those presentations around was never my favourite thing, and when I realized that reveal.js is just html and javascript, I knew I could host it online. Then GitHub pages came to my mind and I tested it and it worked. And that's the story why I host my school presentations here.

Projects

What I am working on

I am working in my freetime on a handful projects. These projects couldn't be more different but that's the kind of person I am. From Minecraft mods to random apps that make my life easier, eveything's there.

Embers' Construct

A Tinkers'-Embers-Bridge

Curseforge Page Github Repository

Looking around I just didn't find the right mod that fulfilled this purpose. Me, having modding experience (although I never finished/published one before) took the task on myself and created Embers' Construct. Although it is still in heavy development I actually released it and updated it several times till now.

AniSync

A MAL to AniList Syncing-App

Trello Board Github Repository

I used MAL before but then a friend came to me and said: "AniList is much better!". I looked and it and answered: "Yup, that's right. But I still will use MAL because I can sync my progress directly with my profile through the application I use to watch my stuff. But I don't want to manually sync those profiles. Ok, I just make an app for it." So time passed and I progressed further and further but because I don't want anyone to die from my app there is no release till now. But to "enjoy" it, you can just compile it yourself via Android Studio.

QuickNeko

A questionable Chrome-Extension

nekos.life Github Repository

This is a silly chrome extension I made in an evening and an afternoon. It fetches random images from nekos.life and displays them in a browser action. You can click the image to go to the next, select different categories, switch between sfw and nsfw and toggle a slideshow that changes every ~5 seconds to a new image.
